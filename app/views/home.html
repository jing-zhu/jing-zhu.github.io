About Me

I am currently a Ph.D. Student in the Department of Civil and Environmental Engineering at Tufts University. I work with Prof. Laurie Baise in the the Geohazards Research Lab. My research focuses on methods development in liquefaction hazard assessment at a global scale and documentation of liquefaction using satellite imagery. I have a broad background in liquefaction, remote sensing, GIS, site response, and ground motion simulation.
